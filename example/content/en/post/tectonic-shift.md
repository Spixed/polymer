We spent a decade removing the "computer" from the screen. We flattened buttons, removed borders, and floated elements in a sea of white space. But the pendulum is swinging back.

### Heavy Borders
The new wave isn't about simulating reality, but about celebrating the digital container. We are seeing pure CMYK values hitting the screen. {{< hl blue >}}Electric Blue{{</hl >}}, {{< hl orange >}}Safety Orange{{</hl >}}, and {{< hl green >}}Signal Green{{</hl >}} are used not just for accents, but for entire backgrounds.
